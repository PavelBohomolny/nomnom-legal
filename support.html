FAQ

  • How do I clear my current week? Open Settings and tap "Clear Current Week Plan".
  • How do I report a bug? Open Settings -> Send Feedback and attach screenshots if needed.
  • How do I change my profile info? Open Account and use Edit Profile.
  • How do I manage premium subscription? Open Settings and tap "Manage Subscription".
  • Why is premium not unlocked? Use "Restore Purchases". If issue remains, contact support and include your app version.
